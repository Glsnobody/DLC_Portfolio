The Eyeliner

The idea of taking a photograph that captured the essence of my younger sister led me to make "The eyeliner." As I wandered through the possible options, I realized that nothing could better define the direct, unfiltered, intimidating, and determined personality that she possesses other than the concept behind the eyeliner. Her expressive face with the knife as a symbol of her intimidating personality along with the eyeliner reminds me of one of the phrases she often says, "Draw the cat eye sharp enough to kill a man."

Coincidence - Not a regular love story

There are love stories that are just around the corner or that happen for no reason, perhaps because they were meant to be. Approximately 3 years ago, through a text message that led us to an extensive debate on problematics and ideologies of the modern society, I managed to coincide with what would be the beginning of a somewhat unusual love story. This gallery was a personal project, to capture the connection and the story behind our love.

Art in a crowded city

New York is a city constantly in motion, and the people who live there move quickly and confidently. However, as they go about their busy day, they don’t always see the beauty of the city. You can find many forms of art walking around New York, whether it’s paintings, clothing, or music there is always something unique to enjoy. The way most New Yorkers seem to miss one of the most beautiful aspects of the city is something I wanted to capture.

Forever home: the memories I’ll always carry

Home is a safe place that allows us to put aside the hardships of life and just relax. Being away from this safe place is hard but it’s also one of the most important steps we take in life as we grow up. A home isn’t perfect, they have flaws and imperfections, but nothing can match those feelings of safety, security, and love when you are there.

Masculinity: Just a social construct

Masculinity is a rigid term used to describe the social expectations of what it means to be a man. Traditionally, men are usually meant to be seen as strong, stoic, and brave. However, This narrow definition of what a man should be is socially constructed and does not accurately describe the various ways in which men can be masculine. I wanted to showcase a man in a more untraditional setting to prove that masculinity is more than what society defines for us.

Skin

Learning to be comfortable in our own bodies is incredibly important for our own self-image. Our skin is the first layer of our body that we can see, and it comes with a variety of different blemishes, patterns, and tones. I wanted to do a photoshoot that encapsulates the beauty of our skin, despite it’s color and any imperfections it may have.

Dominican Republic - Ei' Patio

For Dominicans "Ei Patio" is like a way to say: the place we are from, in this case, Dominican Republic. For this project I went around local places to show people a different side of DR that they normally wouldn't see. I took pictures on digital and also film to create another perspective of my country. As I was driving around, I met with locals that happily gave me the opportunity of photographing them. I hope people enjoy the journey of knowing my country through my lens.

Mood board

I made this mood board for my online digital photography class that I took at Parsons as a way to inspire my future work. I wanted to display elements of femininity, beauty, creativity, confidence, and bright colors.

Beau Ideal

Beauty is a subjective concept. It’s definition varies greatly between people and cultures. Yet there is one thing that increases beauty among any individual, that being confidence. The confidence to be yourself and not care what others think about you can make anyone incredibly beautiful. I want to show people that beauty radiates from within. These photos were inspired by a mood board that I created for my digital photography class that I took at Parsons.

The Road between Lights and Shadows

For my digital photography class I was tasked with capturing lights and shadows working together. I noticed this man crossing the street with this clean line of light peaking through the buildings. I find it very striking how the light cuts across the image and the man is just about to cross over it.

C’est comme ça - Just how it is

These photos were inspired by a dream I had about this chair standing in the water. There was something about the stark contrast of these two things that didn’t go together that heavily inspired me. I drove 4 hours to Monte Cristi to recreate what I had seen in my dream.

“La Doña”

The woman in this photograph is a very well known and loved person from San Francisco de Macorís in The Dominican Republic. She sells coffee and cookies on the same corner every morning. I found it very interesting how this very humble and unassuming lady is a very important part of the daily lives of the people who live there.

Enough?

Beauty standards heavily impact our society. People of all races and genders have been subject to all sorts of impossible beauty standards that destroy our own self-image. The mirror represents the desires and obsessions we have over our own image and the lingering feeling of wanting to be enough to fit into this world.

Solitude

I took these photos on my birthday, and for me they represent finding the comfort in being alone as I move forward in life. I experimented with the lighting and shadows.

Boss

Women have been fighting for equal opportunity for many years, especially when it comes to career advancement. This glass ceiling prevents women from rising to powerful positions and taking on higher responsibilities due to discrimination. “Boss” is my way of showing that powerful woman in positions of power should be normalized.

Pieces of love

Love is one of the strongest emotions we have. It motivates us and helps us grow into better people. Behind every piece of love in this world there is a story about connection, vulnerability, support, safety, and happiness. When people are in love, they can look at each other and see these emotions through their eyes. They have a connection so strong they are able to peer through each other’s souls and see their true emotions.
